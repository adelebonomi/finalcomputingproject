    The Worldly Wine Website!

    Welcome to Grace and Adele's final project! We are so glad you are here. Strap yourself in for a wild ride to learn more about the wines of the world while having fun along the way!

    Project ID: 5

    Wine Production, Consumption and Reviews Across the World

    To better understand wine trends worldwide, we chose to look into wine consumption and production over time. We were particularly interested in the price of specific wines based on location or province. Therefore, we utilized visualizations to unpack the varying differences across regions over time. Additionally, we were intrigued by wine consumption over time based on one's country of origin. The production of certain countries also emerged as a topic to discuss while analyzing our data. Lastly, understanding the quality of wine compared to specific inputs, such as fixed acidity, was a focus of our research.

    Therefore our exploration questions were: In Spain specifically, how does a wine's region of production impact it's price? What countries/regions produce the most wine? In what countries/regions are individuals consuming the most wine? How does fixed acidity relate to wine quality overall? .

    Our data sources

    We found our data from mainly the data source Kaggle. After finding massive data sources with 130,000+ rows, we had to clean several data sets to make them suitable for visualizations and analysis. We specifically focused on Spain regarding wine prices to focus in on a certain region to learn more about wine production. We also used Statistica as a source to understand global wine production and consumption.
